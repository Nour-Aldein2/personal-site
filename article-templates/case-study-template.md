# Case study template

## Problem

State the exact problem, constraint, or task.

## Context and why it matters

Explain why this project matters to users, teams, or the field.

## Data and inputs

Describe the datasets, signals, or system inputs.

## Approach

Explain the architecture, workflow, and major design choices.

## Results

Report outcomes, metrics, and what changed.

## Interpretation and limitations

Discuss what the results mean and what the work does **not** prove.

## Lessons learned

Capture the most reusable takeaways.

## Next steps

List the next practical improvements or follow-up questions.
