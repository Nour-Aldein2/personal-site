# Research note template

## Question

State the specific question or claim under examination.

## Background

Give only the context needed for the note.

## Method or reasoning

Describe the method, experiment, or line of reasoning.

## Findings

Report the main observations or conclusions.

## Implications

Explain why the findings matter.

## Limitations and open questions

Document unresolved uncertainty and next questions.

## References

List the sources, papers, or data used in the note.
